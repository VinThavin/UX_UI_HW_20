The Kindness Project

Redesigned of registration form of NPO the Kindness project.

What is The Kindness Project ?

The Kindness Project is an organization that helps foster parents by supplying clothing, furniture,and other essential items free of charge, but you must be a registered foster parent.

Discovery Step

Research

We conducted five interviews (who are current webpage users) to collect qualitative data from users.

User Key Insights

    “I could not track my progress of filling the form which made it overwhelming.”

    “I did not know how to scan and upload paper documents to the form.”

    “I did not know that there is another form sent to email to be filled after completing the registration form.”

Problem Statement

Registering is easy to do online however the issue most new users face is that they only have one way of accessing the internet, a smart phone, because most users registering do not own a desktop.

Value Prosposition

Our solution is to make it easier for new users to fill out the form on the registration page for the mobile website. By redesigning the webpage to be visually easier for users to understand.
